About SHAHVAR

Luxury Men's Fashion House in Santacruz, Mumbai

Our Story

SHAHVAR is a luxury men's fashion house in Santacruz, Mumbai, specializing in bespoke sherwanis, designer kurtas, Indo-Western ensembles, Bandhgala jackets, Jodhpuri suits, reception suits, tuxedos, and premium wedding wear for the modern gentleman.

Every garment is crafted with exceptional attention to detail, blending traditional Indian craftsmanship with contemporary tailoring, luxurious fabrics, and impeccable finishing. From custom-made groom sherwanis and made-to-measure wedding suits to festive kurta sets, formal wear, and occasion ensembles, SHAHVAR delivers a personalized styling experience for weddings, engagements, receptions, festivals, and celebrations.
